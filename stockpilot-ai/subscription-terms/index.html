StockPilot AI

Subscription Terms

Effective date: June 26, 2026

StockPilot AI Premium is an auto-renewing monthly subscription offered through Apple In-App Purchase.

Plan

StockPilot AI Premium includes a 3-day free trial. After the free trial, the subscription is $1.99 USD per month unless canceled. Pricing shown in the app may vary by region and is provided by Apple's StoreKit product metadata where available.

Billing

Payment is charged to the user's Apple ID. The subscription renews automatically unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period.

Managing or Canceling

Users can manage or cancel subscriptions through Apple account settings. Restore Purchases is available inside the StockPilot AI app.

Terms of Use

StockPilot AI uses Apple's standard Terms of Use (EULA): https://www.apple.com/legal/internet-services/itunes/dev/stdeula/

Educational Use Only

StockPilot AI provides educational market intelligence only. It is not a broker, investment advisor, or financial planner. Nothing in the app constitutes financial advice or guarantees investment performance.
