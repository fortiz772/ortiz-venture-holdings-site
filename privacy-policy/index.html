Chronova AI Privacy Policy

Chronova AI is built by Ortiz Venture Holdings LLC to help users organize goals, schedules, business notes, alerts, weather, and planning tools. This policy explains how the app handles information.

Data Collection

Chronova AI does not collect user data on our servers and does not sell user data. The app stores user-created goals, events, business entries, alerts, settings, weather location, and planner history on the user's device.

Permissions and Optional Services

Calendar, location, notification, weather, Google Calendar, and AI features are used only when the user enables them and grants the required permissions or provides the required credentials.

Local Storage and Credentials

API keys, OAuth tokens, and other sensitive settings are stored on the device using secure device storage when available. Users may remove connected accounts, permissions, or app data through the app settings or iOS settings.

Third-Party Services

When enabled by the user, Chronova AI may contact third-party services such as calendar, weather, news, or AI providers to complete the user's request. These services may process the information needed to provide their feature, such as a location for weather or calendar authorization for calendar sync.

Tracking and Advertising

Chronova AI does not use third-party advertising tracking and does not track users across apps or websites.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy can be sent to cisco@ortizventureholdings.com.
